Honest assessment

What Q1 Did Not Fully Solve

Q1 was a foundation quarter. The work was directionally clear, but the scale of the existing backlog and the limits of the current tracking setup meant not everything could be resolved in one quarter.

  • Large volume of legacy tutorials still in the re-edit or restructuring queue.
  • Many older posts still lack full content, product links, banners, and tracking setup.
  • Current tracking is too partial to measure user journeys end to end with confidence.
  • Telegram distribution workflow is active, but consistent rollout is still being established.
  • Some categories involve heavier technical review, which slows down the improvement cycle.
