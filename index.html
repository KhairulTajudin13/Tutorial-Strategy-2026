The Strategic Objective

My Q1 objective was to take clearer ownership of the Cytron Tutorial platform so it becomes more organized, more consistent, easier to maintain, and easier to evaluate.

Why tutorial ownership matters

Without a clear owner, tutorial quality becomes inconsistent and harder to scale. The WI gives the baseline publishing rules, while the newer template gives writers a clearer flow for building better tutorial pages.

  • Creates a repeatable publishing standard for contributors
  • Improves readability and consistency across legacy and new tutorials
  • Makes future tracking, scaling, and automation more realistic
Open WI summary Open template PDF

Why this supports business value

A good tutorial is more than documentation. It can help users discover products, finish setup with more confidence, reduce support friction, and create assisted-commerce signals when the right products and next steps are easier to act on.

  • Capture problem-led search intent
  • Reduce setup friction and improve user confidence
  • Support product clicks, add-to-cart actions, and inquiry paths

Priority categories

Q1 Challenges

Q1 was a foundation quarter. The challenge was not lack of direction, but the size of the legacy backlog and the limits of the current platform.

  • Large volume of legacy tutorials still needing re-edit or restructuring.
  • Many old posts do not yet have full content completion, product links, banners, or tracking.
  • Tracking is still too partial to measure long user journeys confidently.
  • Distribution support exists, but rollout is still incomplete.
  • Some categories are more complex because they involve heavier technical validation.

My Focus for Q2: Activation & Measurement

Q1 built the foundation. Q2 should make the value more visible.

01

Finish priority migrations

Continue upgrading the most important categories and legacy pages first instead of spreading effort too thin.

02

Strengthen tracking & CTA flow

Improve product links, CTA flow, and attribution setup so tutorial value becomes easier to measure.

03

Expand distribution

Turn automation and channel support into a more repeatable distribution layer for tutorials.

04

Report impact monthly

Start giving management a simpler monthly readout of progress, engagement, search demand, and assisted-commerce signals.

April
Finish priority pages and tighten tracking setup
May
Test distribution flow and category / series visibility
June
Prepare the first clearer impact report for management
